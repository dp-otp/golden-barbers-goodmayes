The Art
of Fades

A fade blends hair from short to shorter seamlessly. The starting point defines the style: low fades keep length, high fades go skin-tight near the crown.

Low Fade
Starts just above the ear. Classic, versatile, suits most face shapes.
Mid Fade
Starts midway up the side. Bold contrast while keeping some length.
High / Skin Fade
Tight, dramatic, starts near the temple. Maximum contrast and definition.

Chapter 4

Beard
Grooming

A well-kept beard isn't just trimmed — it's shaped to your face. Neckline, cheek line and mustache each need individual attention. A barber uses trimmers, a straight razor for definition, and often hot oil.

The Neckline
Two fingers above the Adam's apple. Clean razor edge makes all the difference.
Cheek Line
Follow your natural line or ask for a tidy-up. Avoid going too low — it narrows the face.
Beard Oil
Applied daily to moisturise the skin underneath and keep hair soft and shaped.

Chapter 5

Ear
Flaming

Ear flaming uses a lit wick held near the ear canal to remove unwanted hair safely and painlessly. The heat seals the follicle, reducing regrowth. It takes under 5 minutes and leaves your ears looking sharp and clean.

Golden Barbers are experienced specialists and offer this as a dedicated service.

Chapter 6

Hot Towel
Shave

A hot towel shave is the definitive barbershop ritual. The hot towel opens pores, softening hair and skin. Pre-shave oil is applied, then rich lather, then the straight razor glides with precision. A cold towel closes pores. The result: the closest, smoothest shave possible.

Products &
Daily Routine

The right product makes or breaks a style. Clay = texture and hold. Pomade = shine and slick. Sea salt = natural and messy. Apply to damp hair for better coverage.

Morning
Wet or wash hair, apply product, brush into shape.
Weekly
Deep condition once a week. Apply beard oil nightly.
Every 3–4 Weeks

Chapter 9

Hair Health
& Hair Loss

Hair loss is common and treatable. Early action matters — the sooner you address thinning, the more options you have. The Norwood scale classifies severity from 1–7.

Minoxidil
Topical treatment applied to scalp. Clinically proven to slow loss and regrow hair.
Scalp Micropigmentation
Tattooing technique mimicking a buzz cut on a bald scalp. Instant visual result.
The Right Cut
Skin fades and buzz cuts make thinning hair look intentional and sharp.
